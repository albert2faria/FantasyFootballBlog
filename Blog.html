Fantasy Team Premier League 2020 - 2021 (Season Post-Covid)

Most Important Players and Positions:

Midfielders are the most important position in Fantasy league football and the place where you should spend your money. Example of a player: Aubameyang.

Defense is the place where you can save your budget, with one exception Alexander-Aarnold is a must have.

The Keepers are the position where you can avoid spending the big bucks, keep a solid and trusty goallie with a low cost. Example: Ryan or MCarthy.

Forwards are key in Fantasy Football, spend some money in a power forward and then just use budget picks to fill the rest of them. Example: Sergio Aguero.
